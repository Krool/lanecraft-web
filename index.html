About the Game

Lanecraft is a real-time strategy game where you pick a race, harvest resources, build your base, and send waves of units down lanes to destroy the enemy headquarters. Each of the 9 races plays differently with unique unit stats, abilities, and resource economies.

Play solo against intelligent bot AI that adapts its strategy based on what you build, or go online with friends in 2v2, 3v3, and 4v4 team battles. Research upgrades to unlock powerful unit evolutions with branching skill trees.
